The Problem

Physical Oracle
Problem

01

Blind Tokenization

A smart contract cannot determine whether a building exists, whether its foundation is compromised, or whether the floor area matches the emission prospectus.

02

MiCA Article 36

EU regulation requires proof of physical existence, ongoing verification, and transparent collateral management. Most RWA protocols are not ready.

03

Static Audits Fail

Traditional appraisals capture value at a single point in time. Hidden structural defects, degradation, and floor area manipulation go undetected.

04

$32B at Risk

ERC-3643 secures over $32 billion in tokenized assets — with zero standardized mechanism to verify the physical condition of underlying collateral.

The Solution

IPAS Verification Standard

Five structural nodes. Risk-based inspection schedule. Cryptographic attestations on-chain. Licensed CC3 engineers verify physical assets on-site — results signed, hashed, and pushed to ERC-3643 compliance modules and Chainlink Proof of Reserve.

Smart contracts automatically block trading of degraded assets. The physical oracle problem is solved.
